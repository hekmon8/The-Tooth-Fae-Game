# The Tooth Fae - Game Overview

## Game Summary

**The Tooth Fae** is a psychological horror simulation game that puts players in the role of a mysterious tooth-collecting entity. The game challenges players to navigate homes, extract teeth from sleeping creatures, and build their collection while adhering to strict rules and maintaining absolute stealth.

## Core Concept

Players must collect teeth from fragile beings while following specific rules to prevent their targets from dying. The game creates tension through its stealth mechanics, time pressure, and the constant threat of being discovered.

### The Tagline
*"Collect lovely lovely teeth from fragile things."*

## Gameplay Features

### Core Mechanics

1. **Stealth System**
   - Absolute stealth requirement - players must never be seen
   - Detection results in failure
   - Environmental awareness critical

2. **Tooth Extraction Process**
   - Preparation phase required before extraction
   - Visual indicator (bleeding) signals readiness
   - Improper timing can result in target death

3. **Time Pressure**
   - Limited time to complete extractions
   - Fragile targets can die from fright
   - Balance between speed and caution required

4. **Collection Management**
   - Town screen for collection viewing
   - Progress tracking (warning: back button clears progress)
   - Multiple targets across different locations

### The Rules of Collection

Players must follow these cardinal rules:

> **Rule 1**: Pulling teeth requires preparation, otherwise a thing may die.

> **Rule 2**: A tooth that bleeds is ripe for plucking.

> **Rule 3**: Don't dally. Even such a thing as a fright might be too much for them.

> **Rule 4**: **YOU MUST NEVER BE SEEN.**
