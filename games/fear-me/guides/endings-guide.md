# Fear Me Endings Guide

Fear Me features 9 unique endings that vary based on your dialogue choices and affection levels throughout the game. This comprehensive guide explains the different ending categories, their requirements, and strategies for discovering all possible conclusions in this psychological horror visual novel.

## Understanding Ending Categories

The 9 endings in Fear Me fall into three main categories: Survival Endings, Romance Endings, and Secret Endings. Each category requires different approaches to dialogue choices and affection management.

### Survival Endings

Survival endings prioritize escaping the dangerous situation safely over building romantic connections. These conclusions focus on self-preservation and typically require:

- **Lower to medium affection levels** with all characters
- **Cautious dialogue choices** that prioritize safety over intimacy
- **Self-protective responses** when faced with risky situations
- **Maintaining emotional distance** from dangerous characters

To achieve survival endings, **[play Fear Me online free](https://playthetoothfae.com/games/fearme)** and focus on choices that demonstrate self-awareness about the danger you're in. Avoid romantic advances and prioritize escape opportunities when they present themselves.

### Romance Endings

Romance endings explore deeper connections with your chosen love interest. These conclusions require significant investment in a single character's route and demand:

- **High affection levels** with your chosen romantic interest (typically 70+ points)
- **Consistent commitment** to one character throughout the playthrough
- **Romantic dialogue choices** that show interest and emotional connection
- **Understanding of character psychology** and what they need emotionally

Each of the three romantic interests (Jeff the Killer, male character, female character) has unique romance endings that reflect their personality and relationship dynamics. Building maximum affection while making choices that align with their values unlocks these intimate conclusions.

### Secret Endings

Secret endings require specific combinations of choices across multiple chapters that may not be immediately obvious. These special conclusions often:

- **Reward unconventional thinking** and choices that diverge from obvious paths
- **Require specific affection ranges** that aren't purely high or low
- **Unlock through particular dialogue combinations** across different story sections
- **Reveal hidden story aspects** not accessible in standard endings

Discovering secret endings typically requires experimentation, note-taking, and willingness to try unexpected dialogue options. **[Learn how to play Fear Me](https://playthetoothfae.com/games/fearme#endings-guide)** thoroughly to identify the subtle hints that point toward these hidden conclusions.

## Affection Requirements for Endings

Different endings activate at specific affection thresholds with each character. Understanding these requirements helps you target specific conclusions efficiently:

### High Affection Range (70-100 points)
- Primary romance endings for each character
- Deep emotional connection conclusions
- Most positive romantic outcomes

### Medium Affection Range (40-69 points)
- Mixed endings with ambiguous outcomes
- Partial romance conclusions
- Some secret ending triggers

### Low Affection Range (0-39 points)
- Survival-focused endings
- Escape-oriented conclusions
- Some character-specific bad endings

### Negative Affection (Below 0 points)
- Bad endings with negative outcomes
- Consequences for antagonizing characters
- Dangerous conclusion scenarios

## Character-Specific Romance Routes

### Jeff the Killer Route

Jeff's romance route offers the most intense psychological thriller elements combined with twisted romance possibilities. To achieve his romance endings:

1. **Demonstrate understanding** of his complex psychology
2. **Show courage** rather than excessive fear
3. **Make bold choices** that intrigue rather than bore him
4. **Balance submission with independence** - he respects strength
5. **Commit exclusively** to his route without pursuing other characters

### Male Character Route

The male character route provides a different narrative experience with unique personality traits. Success on this path requires:

1. **Identify his core values** through early dialogue
2. **Make choices** that align with his personality preferences
3. **Build trust gradually** through consistent responses
4. **Focus affection-building** exclusively on him

### Female Character Route

The female character route offers alternative perspectives on the Fear Me story. To unlock her romance endings:

1. **Pay attention** to her emotional needs
2. **Choose dialogue** that demonstrates empathy and connection
3. **Avoid pursuing** other characters simultaneously
4. **Maintain high affection** through appropriate responses
