## Common Pitfalls and Solutions

**Rushing Extraction**: The most common beginner mistake is attempting extraction before preparation completes, resulting in target death. Solution: Force yourself to wait for the bleeding indicator. Count preparation time if needed during learning phase. Train patience through deliberate practice. Use visual confirmation before every extraction.

**Poor Stealth Discipline**: Getting careless with stealth after early successes leads to sudden failures. Solution: Recommit to stealth-first mindset before every extraction. Slow down approach speed when you notice rushing. Increase observation time before movements. Review stealth fundamentals regularly.

**Panic Under Pressure**: Making mistakes when stressed undermines your skills. Solution: Take deep breaths when you feel panic rising. Slow down your thought process consciously. Focus on fundamentals rather than complex strategies. Accept that failure is acceptable - this reduces pressure.
