# Endings Guide - The Tooth Fae

## Perfect Run Goals

Achieving a perfect collection in The Tooth Fae represents the ultimate mastery challenge. [Play The Tooth Fae online](https://playthetoothfae.com/games/tooth-fae) to pursue completion goals that test your skills, patience, and dedication to the art of tooth collection.

## Complete Collection Objectives

### Full Collection Target

The perfect run requires collecting teeth from all available targets without failures. Every location must be visited and every extraction must succeed. Zero failed attempts maintain your perfect record. Absolute stealth must be maintained throughout the entire playthrough.

Track your collection progress carefully on the town screen. Note which targets you've completed successfully. Plan efficient routes to minimize backtracking. Avoid the back button to preserve hard-earned progress.

### Zero Failure Challenge

A true perfect run tolerates no mistakes. Never trigger detection from targets. Always execute extraction at the perfect moment. Complete every extraction on the first attempt. Maintain composure under pressure throughout all missions.

This challenge demands mastery of all game mechanics. Your stealth navigation must be flawless. Extraction timing must be precise every single time. Risk assessment prevents attempting extractions under poor conditions. Mental resilience handles the pressure of maintaining perfection.

## Mental Preparation for Perfect Runs

### Pressure Management

Perfect run attempts create intense pressure as progress accumulates. Stay calm during late-game extractions when perfection is at stake. Don't let fear of failure cause mistakes. Treat each extraction identically regardless of streak status.

Take breaks between difficult extractions if stress builds. Step away from the game if frustration mounts. Return with fresh focus rather than pushing through fatigue. Mental state directly impacts performance quality.

### Learning from Near-Perfect Runs

Failed perfect runs teach valuable lessons. Analyze what caused the failure - stealth error, timing mistake, or judgment lapse. Identify patterns in your failures to address weaknesses. Each failed perfect run builds skills for the next attempt.

Don't view failed perfect runs as wasted time. The experience gained from 95% completion runs is invaluable. Skills developed during long perfect streaks transfer to future attempts. Persistence eventually yields success.
