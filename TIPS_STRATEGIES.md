# Tips & Strategies for The Tooth Fae

## Beginner Tips

### First Steps

**Start Slow**
- Don't rush your first playthrough
- Take time to learn the mechanics
- Failures are part of the learning process
- Each attempt teaches you something new

**Master the Basics**
- Understand all four cardinal rules
- Practice stealth navigation
- Learn to recognize the bleeding indicator
- Get comfortable with your chosen input method

**Use Sound Effectively**
- Always play with sound enabled
- Listen for audio cues
- Environmental sounds provide information
- Music changes signal game states

### Understanding the Rules

**Rule 1: Preparation**
- Never skip the preparation phase
- Rushing equals target death
- Patience is essential
- Wait for complete preparation

**Rule 2: Bleeding Signal**
- Most critical visual indicator
- Only extract when bleeding appears
- Premature extraction fails
- Train your eyes to spot it quickly

**Rule 3: Time Management**
- Balance speed with caution
- Don't panic under pressure
- Efficiency improves with practice
- Learn optimal timing windows

**Rule 4: Stealth**
- Absolute stealth is non-negotiable
- One detection = game over
- Always prioritize staying hidden
- When in doubt, wait and observe

## Common Pitfalls & Solutions

### Mistake: Rushing Extraction

**Problem**: Attempting extraction too early
**Symptoms**: Target dies, mission fails
**Solution**:
- Force yourself to wait for bleeding
- Count preparation time if needed
- Train patience through practice
- Use visual confirmation

### Mistake: Poor Stealth Discipline

**Problem**: Getting careless with stealth
**Symptoms**: Frequent detection, failed missions
**Solution**:
- Recommit to stealth-first mindset
- Slow down approach speed
- Increase observation time
- Review stealth fundamentals

### Mistake: Panic Under Pressure

**Problem**: Making mistakes when stressed
**Symptoms**: Failed extractions, detection errors
**Solution**:
- Take deep breaths
- Slow down thought process
- Focus on fundamentals
- Accept failure as learning

### Mistake: Ignoring Audio Cues

**Problem**: Playing without sound or not listening
**Symptoms**: Missing important information
**Solution**:
- Always enable sound
- Use headphones
- Listen actively
- Learn what each sound means

### Mistake: Back Button Usage

**Problem**: Accidentally clearing progress
**Symptoms**: Lost collection
**Solution**:
- Develop habit of never using back
- Use in-game navigation only
- Be deliberate with all inputs
- Stay focused on UI
